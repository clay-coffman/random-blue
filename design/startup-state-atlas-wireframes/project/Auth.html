Auth, onboarding & admin ~ wireframes ~

Two user types: normal (founders, business owners, investors — pick one at signup; capture role-specific info during onboarding) and admin (Utah GOEO team; promoted manually, separate dashboard). Auth is email + password with magic-link fallback. Verification by email. Domain-email check still gates company claims, layered on top of the regular account.
